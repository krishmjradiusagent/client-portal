# Radius Role Matrix

| Role | Can Do | Cannot Do / Limits |
|---|---|---|
| Agent | Manage own clients, transactions, offers, listings, envelopes | Cannot manage full team unless given role |
| Team Lead | View/manage team workflows, assign team members, oversee transactions | Should not lose visibility into team-wide work |
| Team Member | Work on assigned clients/transactions | Limited by team permission |
| TC | Work on assigned transactions, support agents/teams | Should only see assigned scope |
| Admin | Operational/config support | Needs clarity on system-level actions |

## Role Rule

If feature affects permissions, include:
- who can see it
- who can edit it
- who gets notified
- what happens for no access
